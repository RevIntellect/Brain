## Bulleted Summary of Setup Actions

- **Purchased a Mac Mini** to run the Open Claw AI agent separately from the main laptop to avoid security risks such as unauthorized access to emails or bank accounts [(00:00:10)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cJD3LBIECQc&t=10)

- **Installed Open Claw** on the Mac Mini and granted it full access to control the computer [(00:00:20)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cJD3LBIECQc&t=20)

- **Tested a simple task** by instructing the AI agent to play the song "Benz by Mazi" in 4K quality on YouTube; the agent successfully searched, played, stopped, adjusted quality, and resumed playback [(00:00:40)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cJD3LBIECQc&t=40)

- **Demonstrated remote control** of the AI agent via Telegram, highlighting the convenience of issuing commands from anywhere [(00:01:10)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cJD3LBIECQc&t=70)

- **Performed video editing task** by instructing the AI to clip 30 seconds of a video and save it to the desktop [(00:01:40)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cJD3LBIECQc&t=100)

- **Outlined future goals** to improve security and expand automation capabilities, including managing emails and full voice command control via Telegram [(00:02:00)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cJD3LBIECQc&t=120)

## Key Takeaways

This setup showcases the initial successful integration of Open Claw on a dedicated Mac Mini, emphasizing security and remote automation. The dedicated hardware approach isolates the AI agent from sensitive personal data on the main machine.
